My Favorite Chocolate Chip Cookies

This chocolate chip cookie recipe will change your life. I have been on the hunt for a easy, yet delicious chocolate chip cookie recipe and this is it. The cookies have a nice crispy outside with a soft and chewy inside. The best part is that this recipe does not require chilling the cookie dough, so you'll be able to bake and eat them much quicker! For additional details, visit The Best Chocolate Chip Cookies.

Ingredients

For this recipe, you will need a few simple ingredients.

  • 1/2 Cup Unsalted Butter (melted and cooled)
  • 1/2 Cup Light Brown Sugar (packed)
  • 1/3 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 + 1/4 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 3/4 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Cup to 1 + 1/2 Cup Chocolate Chips (can adjust the amount of chocolate chips to your liking)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Farenheit and line your cookie pan.
  2. Melt the butter and let it sit until it has cooled.
  3. While the butter is cooling, combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a mixing bowl.
  4. In a seperate mixing bowl, whisk the melted and cooled butter, light brown sugar, and granulated sugar until combined. Then add in the egg and vanilla extract and whisk once again until combined.
  5. Add in the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and use a rubber spatula to combine. Finally, add in the chocolate chips and mix the batter until the chocolate chips are distributed.
  6. Using a scoop, scoop and place the cookie dough onto your tray and bake for approximately 11 to 13 minutes.
  7. Once the cookies are done, let them rest and cool. After letting them cool, simply eat and enjoy!
